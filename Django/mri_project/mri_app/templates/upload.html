Description

Heart disease detection using medical imaging involves analyzing echocardiograms, CT scans, and MRI images to identify abnormalities in heart structure and function. AI-driven analysis assists healthcare professionals in diagnosing cardiovascular conditions early, improving treatment outcomes.

Symptoms

Common indicators of heart disease include chest pain, shortness of breath, irregular heartbeat, and fatigue. AI-powered image analysis can detect early signs such as arterial blockages, reduced heart function, and structural anomalies before symptoms become severe.

Advantages

AI-driven heart disease detection enhances accuracy in identifying cardiovascular risks. It reduces manual interpretation errors, enables early intervention, and assists cardiologists in making precise diagnoses through automated image analysis.

Challenges

Challenges include ensuring high-quality imaging, variations in heart conditions across patients, and maintaining AI model reliability. Ethical concerns related to patient data privacy and the need for expert validation also remain critical.
